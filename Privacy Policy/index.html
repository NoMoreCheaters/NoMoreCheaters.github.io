Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 31, 2026

1. Complete Local Client-Side Processing

This website functions entirely on the client side (inside your web browser). Any data you type into the generator—such as target player usernames, video evidence links, server IDs, and platform types—is rendered locally in real-time. We do not run databases or tracking algorithms to intercept, view, or log what you type.

2. Local Storage Technology

To improve your experience, the "Report History" section utilizes your browser's "localStorage" technology. This saves a small text file directly onto your own machine so you can review past reports. This data is never uploaded to the internet and can be completely wiped at any second by using the "Clear All" button.

Our utility contains redirect links to external sites, including official CubeCraft Games pages and feedback forms. Once you leave our site, we have no control over the privacy practices or contents of those external platforms. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party sites you visit.
