How to Use

This tool helps you quickly format player reports for the CubeCraft forums in just a few simple steps.

  1. STEP 1 Fill in the Information

    Enter the required details such as the username(s), date & time, offence type, and video evidence URL. You can use the Copy and Paste buttons next to the fields for faster input.

  2. STEP 2 Add Timestamps

    Specify exactly when the rule-breaking behavior occurs in your video. Click ＋ Add Timestamp to add rows. Select a reason from the dropdown, or choose "Other" to type in your own custom details.

  3. STEP 3 Copy the Formatted Text

    Check the automatically generated text in the 【Preview】 section at the bottom. If everything looks correct, click the Copy All & Save History button. This copies the text to your clipboard and logs it in your history.

  4. STEP 4 Submit on the Forum

    Click 🌐 Open CubeCraft Report Page to head directly to the official forums. Simply paste your copied text (Ctrl+V) into your new forum thread and submit your report.

📂 About the History Feature

Every time you copy a report, it is saved automatically in the "History" section below the form. The history is stored locally in your browser, allowing you to generate multiple report texts sequentially and post them all at once later.

⚠️ Important Note
According to CubeCraft official guidelines, edited evidence videos (including cuts, speed adjustments, or zooms) are highly likely to be rejected. It is strongly recommended to provide raw, unedited video URLs.
